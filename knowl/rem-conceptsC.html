Remark 1.3.0.6.

  • Robust statistics. Two concepts constitute the Robust statistics: Median and IQR. These have little effect when there are extreme observations.
  • Transformation. It is a recalibration of data through a mathematical function, this usually works to rescale the data a make easier to build data models. Some transformation examples are \(x\to\log(), x\to\ln(), x\to\sqrt{x},x\to x^{-1}\)
